 about tuffy

Many years ago, the Chapmans were skeptical about getting a dog. However, as soon as they met Tuffy, they were smitten. Even as a little puppy, his personality shone through.

Tuffy now enjoys a life of happiness and companionship. If by chance you happen to be looking for Tuffy, he is probably with his beloved family.
